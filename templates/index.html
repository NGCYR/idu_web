What Are We Doing?

Now in the world, 3 Dimensional Graphics is everywhere. In phones, computers, movies, games, etc. Literally, we live in a 3D world. But 3D (Especially digitally) lack understandings in a theoretcial manner. It is the job of our IDU to bring light to this controversial but not so well known topic. This website and it's features is a visualisation of 3D transformations, which is one of the more controversial knowledges in 3D Graphics.

Contents

Translation
Shifting the object

Translation is the shift of a graph/shape in the x-axis direction or the y-axis direction, or even both.

Go

Reflection
Mirroring the object over an axis

Reflection is the action of flipping something over an axis. It is the action of mirroring the graph/object.

Go

Dilation
An object squeezing or streching

Dilation is the action or condition of becoming or being made wider, larger, or more open. To put this in simple words, Dilation is simply streching or squeezing graphs/shapes.

Go

Rotatation
An object spinning about a point

Rotation is a motion of a certain space that preserves at least one point. To put this simply, it is objects rotating about a specific point.
